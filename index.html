About me

My name is Shrihan Tadimeti. I'm a dedicated soccer player who has competed at elite levels including ECNL and MLS Next. Growing up in the game shaped how I see the world — I've learned to read a situation before it develops and stay focused under pressure.

Beyond athletics, I'm deeply passionate about sports photography. Being an athlete myself gives me a different perspective behind the lens — I know what a moment feels like before it happens, and I try to capture the story inside it, not just the action on the surface.

I'm also genuinely interested in finance and how markets and businesses work. Whether I'm on the field, behind a camera, or studying how money moves, I bring the same focus and drive to everything I do.
